## 🧠 Welcome Lys Players! (FAQ & Important Info)

### 1. General Info & Your First Day

Welcome to the Lab! We are thrilled to have you participating in our research. On your very first day of data collection, there is no need to stress—we will sit down with you and explain the entire process in detail so you know exactly what to expect. We will also have a couple of printed forms for you to sign (our [project consent form](https://drive.google.com/file/d/10Z115oiV_53LRTJZJuR_zoD2GuKIzHlh/view?usp=sharing) and a [GDPR consent form](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1brGaZzPLNqKugTsjMK07g_F6AU1VemOh/view?usp=sharing)) just to ensure we are strictly adhering to EU data protection standards and keeping your information safe. Additionally, we will sign a citizen’s contract that establishes payment procedure, timing, etc., that we will give you in the office to sign together and provide you with one of the copies.

The device we use is called fNIRS and it records brain activity - with light! This is the same light that the Sun shines on you, so it even has some positive effects on cell metabolism. Sessions with it are kept to be about 20 mins with breaks 5-20 in between sessions. In different sessions you can do different studies! 

How to schedule: We ask that you select which days and times you will be coming in to collect data on this website: [scheduling.lys.inc](https://scheduling.lys.inc/), this will send you (and us) a calendar invite for that time. This system is for the whole time you will be with us on the day, not for each separate session. If you need to reschedule, or you are running late you can email us @: [players@lysneuro.com](mailto:players@lysneuro.com) 

Lunch & Fridge: If you will be in the office during lunchtime, you are more than welcome to bring food and store it in our shared fridge.

Pre-Session Diet (Important!): Please do not consume energy drinks or excessive amounts of sugar right before coming in for data collection. Not only is it unhealthy, but it actually scrambles the brain data we are trying to collect! 

Payment Details: You will be paid about 20 euro/hour of data, which can increase to 25euro if you agree to have a [brain image taken](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1nHl0eSiYS94jgXl9exUkmkVHoCi56xrgDaItsMiDzzQ/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.hzvnqx5fhdad). We usually pay in chunks of 20-25 hours of recording. These numbers are approximate as they depend heavily on income, taxation rates, and other personal details.                                                                                                                 BONUS: For every 100h you record we have a bonus of 5000 euros, and for every 1000h we offer a bonus of 50,000 euros (based on eligibility to collect for these bonuses).

### 2. Study Descriptions

Your time each visit will be divided between the different experiments you want to take part in (provided your eligibility, for example: languages you speak).

### The Audiobook/Podcast Study

- The Task: For this experiment, you will be listening to podcasts or audiobooks.
    
- Choose Your Audio: It is vital for our analysis that you pay close attention and aren't distracted. For this reason, we want you to pick what you listen to! Ensure that the diction is clear in the audio. 
    
- File Length: Make sure you provide enough audio for your time slot! For example, if you are scheduled for a 2-hour recording session, please ensure you have at least 2 hours of .mp3 files ready to go. For example a 2h podcast would get chunked into 20 minute parts that you listen to one at a time.
    
- Language Requirements: Audio can be in any language you are completely fluent in (though we have a slight preference for English).
    
- Action Required Before Your Session: Please share the .mp3 files of the audio you want to listen to at least 1 day before you arrive. 




### The Game Study

- The Task: For this experiment, you will be playing an old-school video game.
    
- Language Requirements: You have to be completely fluent in English

  

### The Imagination Study

- The Task: In this experiment you will be telling us about your imagination. You imagine something for a few seconds, and then write out what you imagined.
    
- Language Requirements: None
    
- Action Required Before Your Session: A pre-recording session in which we train you a bit on this task and see how your brain deals with it. Participation in meditation class with a world class meditation teacher who works with the Lys team, to help you get more easily into the flow of this task, as it can be challenging sometimes to observe your thoughts. 
    
- If you are interested in this task, speak to us directly or emails us at [players@lysneuro.com](mailto:players@lysneuro.com)
