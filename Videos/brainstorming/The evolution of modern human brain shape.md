## Summary
The evolutionary process leading to human brain globularity was gradual and paralleled the emergence of behavioral modernity.

## Key points
- Modern human brain globularity evolved gradually rather than through a single rapid transition
- Brain shape changes paralleled the emergence of behavioral modernity in the archaeological record
- Prefrontal cortex expansion was a key component of this evolutionary shift
- Analysis uses 3D morphometric comparison of fossil and modern human endocasts
