## Summary
This paper investigates how reward model performance scales with model size and training data in RLHF settings, providing empirical insights into the relationship between reward model capacity and downstream RL performance.

## Key points
- Analyzes scaling laws for reward models across different model sizes and dataset sizes
- Shows how reward model quality impacts final policy performance in RLHF
- Provides practical guidance for resource allocation in RLHF training pipelines
- Demonstrates non-trivial interactions between model capacity and training data in reward modeling
