## Summary
This paper proposes MSTFDN, a spatial-temporal fusion decoding network that integrates EEG and fNIRS multimodal data for personalized multi-task brain-computer interface scenarios. The model achieves state-of-the-art performance on available public datasets through sophisticated fusion architecture.

## Key points
- Multimodal fusion of EEG and fNIRS signals for improved brain activity decoding
- Spatial-temporal architecture designed for personalized multi-task scenarios
- Achieves state-of-the-art results on public benchmark datasets
- Demonstrates robustness across subjects with personalization capabilities
