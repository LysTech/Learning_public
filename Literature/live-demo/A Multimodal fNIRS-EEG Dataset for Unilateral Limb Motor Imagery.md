## Summary
This paper presents a multimodal dataset combining fNIRS and EEG recordings during unilateral limb motor imagery tasks with four directional movements. The dataset is publicly available and designed to support research in brain-computer interfaces and motor imagery classification.

## Key points
- Multimodal dataset combining fNIRS and EEG for motor imagery research
- Focuses on unilateral limb motor imagery with four directional movement classes
- Publicly available dataset to support reproducible BCI research
- Addresses the challenge of improving classification accuracy in motor imagery tasks
