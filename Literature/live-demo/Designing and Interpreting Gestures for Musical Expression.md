## Summary
This paper explores the design and interpretation of gesture-based interfaces for musical expression, examining how users can intuitively control musical parameters through physical gestures and how systems can recognize and respond to these gestures.

## Key points
- Gesture design principles for intuitive musical control
- Recognition and interpretation of musical gestures
- Human factors in designing gesture-based music interfaces
- Evaluation of gesture expressiveness for musical applications
