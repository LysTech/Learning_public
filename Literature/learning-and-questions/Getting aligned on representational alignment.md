## Summary
A comprehensive review paper examining the concept of representational alignment across neuroscience and machine learning. The paper synthesizes different definitions and approaches to understanding alignment between neural representations, discussing implications for neuroscience, AI, and cognitive science.

## Key points
- Clarifies and synthesizes multiple definitions of representational alignment used across disciplines
- Reviews methodological approaches for measuring alignment between neural representations in brains and models
- Discusses the relationship between alignment and generalization, transfer learning, and interpretability
- Addresses open questions about what alignment implies for understanding intelligence and brain function
- Provides a unifying framework for thinking about representation learning across neuroscience and machine learning
