## Summary
This work introduces Framed RSA, an extension of representational similarity analysis that preserves both representational geometry and regional mean response preferences in neural data. The method addresses limitations of standard RSA by accounting for regional-mean response patterns alongside similarity structure.

## Key points
- Extends representational similarity analysis (RSA) to honor both geometry and regional-mean response properties
- Addresses a key limitation of standard RSA which discards mean response information
- Provides improved analysis of neural representations across brain regions
- Published at Cognitive Neuroscience Society 2025 conference
