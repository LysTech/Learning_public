## Summary
This work proposes a method to decode fMRI brain scans into words by directly classifying brain activity patterns within a fixed vocabulary. The approach uses decoded words to guide language generation with GPT-2, achieving competitive accuracy on unseen subjects and advancing brain-to-text decoding.

## Key points
- Direct classification of fMRI scans to words within a fixed vocabulary
- Generalizes to previously unseen subjects for realistic brain decoding
- Integrates decoded words with GPT-2 for coherent language generation
- Demonstrates feasibility of decoding natural language from brain activity
