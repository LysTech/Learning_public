Create the world's most accurate visual representations of the jhana states. Prizes for each individual jhana plus a grand prize for best overall contribution.

- Competition focused on creating visual representations of jhana states
- Organized by QRI (Qualia Research Institute) with prizes for each jhana level
- Encourages interdisciplinary approaches combining contemplative knowledge and visualization design
- Aimed at advancing understanding of altered states of consciousness through visual communication
