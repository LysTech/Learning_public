## Summary
This paper proposes brain-tuning, a parameter-efficient fine-tuning method for aligning speech models with brain activity data. The approach improves generalization across subjects and reduces computational requirements compared to full model fine-tuning.

## Key points
- Introduces brain-tuning as a parameter-efficient alternative to full fine-tuning for brain alignment in speech models
- Demonstrates improved generalization performance across different subjects and datasets
- Reduces computational and memory requirements while maintaining or improving alignment quality
- Shows that brain-aligned models can transfer knowledge about neural representations across subjects
