## Summary
This paper investigates the phenomenon of 'brain misalignment' in language models, examining how and why language models can lose coherence and produce nonsensical outputs, with implications for understanding model robustness and alignment.

## Key points
- Explores the concept of 'brain misalignment' as a failure mode in language models
- Analyzes conditions under which language models lose semantic coherence
- Investigates consequences of misalignment for model reliability and safety
- Proposes diagnostic methods to detect and measure brain misalignment
