## Summary
A comprehensive preprint by Rik Henson on Statistical Parametric Mapping (SPM), covering the theoretical foundations and practical applications of SPM for analyzing functional brain imaging data, particularly fMRI.

## Key points
- Introduction to Statistical Parametric Mapping methodology for fMRI analysis
- Practical guidelines for preprocessing and statistical analysis of brain images
- Theoretical framework for mass-univariate approaches in neuroimaging
- Coverage of design considerations and interpretation of SPM results
