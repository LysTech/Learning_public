## Summary
This work investigates the application of transformer models for decoding neural activity across hierarchically organized brain regions. The study demonstrates how transformer architectures can effectively capture relationships between neural signals from different levels of cortical hierarchy and improve neural decoding performance.

## Key points
- Applies transformer models to neural decoding tasks across multiple hierarchical brain regions
- Demonstrates transformers can capture dependencies between neural signals at different hierarchical levels
- Evaluates performance gains over traditional neural decoding methods
- Provides insights into how modern deep learning architectures handle structured brain data
