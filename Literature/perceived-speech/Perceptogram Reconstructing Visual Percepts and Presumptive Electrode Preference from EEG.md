## Summary
This paper presents Perceptogram, a method for reconstructing visual percepts from EEG signals and identifying electrode preferences for specific visual features. The approach combines deep learning with neuroscience principles to decode visual information directly from brain activity and provide interpretability into which electrodes contribute to perception of different visual elements.

## Key points
- Reconstructs visual percepts from EEG signals using neural decoding
- Identifies electrode preferences for specific visual features and perceptual attributes
- Provides interpretable analysis of which brain regions contribute to visual processing
- Demonstrates feasibility of EEG-based visual reconstruction at scale
