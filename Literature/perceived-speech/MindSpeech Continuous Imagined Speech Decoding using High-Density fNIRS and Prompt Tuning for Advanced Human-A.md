## Summary
MindSpeech presents a method for decoding continuous imagined speech directly from brain signals using high-density functional near-infrared spectroscopy (fNIRS). The approach leverages prompt tuning with large language models to enable advanced human-AI interaction without requiring overt speech production.

## Key points
- Decodes continuous imagined speech from high-density fNIRS brain signals without requiring actual vocalization
- Integrates prompt tuning with large language models for improved decoding and human-AI interaction
- Demonstrates the feasibility of non-invasive brain-computer interfaces for speech-based applications
- Advances silent communication and assistive technology for individuals with speech disabilities
