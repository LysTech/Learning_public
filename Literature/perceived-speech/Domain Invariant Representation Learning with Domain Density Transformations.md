## Summary
This paper proposes domain invariant representation learning using domain density transformations to address the domain gap problem where validation and training distributions diverge. The method aims to align representations across domains through learned density transformations, enabling better generalization in domain adaptation scenarios.

## Key points
- Addresses domain gap by learning domain-invariant representations through density transformations
- Proposes a method to bring training and validation distributions into alignment
- Applicable to adversarial domain adaptation and transfer learning settings
- Demonstrates improved generalization across domain shifts
