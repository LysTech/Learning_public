## Summary
This paper examines scaling laws and emergent abilities in large language models, investigating how model capabilities evolve with increases in scale and parameter count.

## Key points
- Analysis of scaling laws in language model performance
- Investigation of emergent abilities that appear at certain scale thresholds
- Empirical evaluation across multiple model sizes and training regimes
- Implications for predicting and understanding capabilities of larger models
