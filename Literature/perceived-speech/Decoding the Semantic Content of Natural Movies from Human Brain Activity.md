## Summary

## Key points
- Successfully decodes semantic features of natural movies from fMRI activity in visual and language areas
- Validates quantitative computational models of semantic representation in the human brain
- Uses voxel-wise encoding models trained on brain responses to novel stimuli
- Demonstrates that semantic information is distributed across visual and temporal cortex during natural movie viewing
- Provides strong evidence that brain activity during passive viewing reflects explicit semantic processing
