## Summary
DeepSeekMath demonstrates that open-source language models can achieve competitive mathematical reasoning capabilities through specialized training on mathematical content and process supervision. The work presents a 7B parameter model that outperforms larger models on mathematical benchmarks, establishing new baselines for open-source mathematical reasoning.

## Key points
- Open-source 7B model achieves strong performance on mathematical reasoning tasks, competing with much larger proprietary models
- Training strategy combines mathematical corpus curation with process supervision and reinforcement learning
- Demonstrates that model scale alone is not the limiting factor for mathematical reasoning in open models
- Provides insights into effective training methodologies for improving reasoning in language models
