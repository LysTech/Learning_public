## Summary
This paper addresses methods for analyzing cortical surface data, focusing on the integration of MNI volumetric space with FreeSurfer surface reconstructions for improved neuroimaging analysis workflows.

## Key points
- Methods for integrating MNI volumetric coordinates with FreeSurfer surface models
- Improved accuracy for surface-based cortical attribute mapping
- Practical workflow for handling volumetric and surface-based data formats
- Relevant for neuroimaging studies requiring both volume and surface analyses
