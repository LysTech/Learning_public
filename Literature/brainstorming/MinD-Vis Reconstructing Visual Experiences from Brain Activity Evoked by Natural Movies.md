## Summary
MinD-Vis presents a method for reconstructing visual images from brain activity (fMRI) evoked by natural movie stimuli. The approach uses diffusion models conditioned on brain embeddings to generate realistic visual reconstructions that match the visual content perceived by subjects.

## Key points
- Reconstructs visual images from fMRI brain activity using diffusion models
- Achieves high-quality reconstructions of natural movie content from brain signals
- Demonstrates that visual reconstruction is possible without task-specific training data
- Provides insights into the relationship between brain activity and visual perception
