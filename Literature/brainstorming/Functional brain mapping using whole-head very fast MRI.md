## Summary
This paper presents advances in functional brain mapping using ultra-fast whole-head MRI techniques. The work demonstrates the application of very fast imaging protocols for capturing brain activation patterns with improved temporal and spatial resolution.

## Key points
- Novel whole-head MRI protocols for rapid functional brain mapping
- Improved temporal resolution for detecting brain activity
- Applications to clinical and research neuroimaging
- Enhanced spatial coverage compared to conventional fMRI approaches
