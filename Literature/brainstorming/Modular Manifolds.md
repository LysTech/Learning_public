## Summary
A geometric framework for co-designing neural net optimizers with manifold constraints.

## Key points
- Proposes a manifold-constrained optimization framework using dissimilarity representation matrices
- Investigates whether geometric constraints on optimizer trajectories improve computational efficiency
- Extends classical optimizer design (e.g., Adam) to operate within structured manifold spaces
- Combines geometric optimization theory with practical neural network training
