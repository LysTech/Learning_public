## Summary
A comprehensive review of deep learning methods applied to EEG-based brain-computer interfaces, covering architectures, applications, and challenges in translating these systems to real-world deployment.

## Key points
- Systematic overview of deep learning architectures (CNNs, RNNs, Transformers) for EEG signal processing
- Discussion of major BCI applications including motor imagery, P300, and SSVEP paradigms
- Analysis of challenges in model generalization and cross-subject/cross-session variability
- Comparison of performance metrics and practical considerations for BCI system deployment
