## Summary
This paper proposes a hybrid approach combining reinforcement learning and diffusion models to enhance EEG signal generation. The method leverages RL to optimize the diffusion process for generating realistic and physiologically plausible EEG signals, addressing limitations in existing generative approaches.

## Key points
- Hybrid framework integrating reinforcement learning with diffusion models for EEG synthesis
- Uses RL to optimize the diffusion sampling process for improved signal quality
- Generates physiologically plausible EEG signals with enhanced fidelity
- Addresses data scarcity challenges in EEG-based brain-computer interface applications
- Demonstrates improvements over baseline diffusion-only approaches
