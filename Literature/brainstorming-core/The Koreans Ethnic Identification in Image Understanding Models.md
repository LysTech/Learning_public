## Summary
This paper investigates how vision-language models identify and represent Korean people in images, examining potential biases and disparities in ethnic recognition across different model architectures and datasets.

## Key points
- Analyzes ethnic identification accuracy and biases in vision-language models for Korean subjects
- Evaluates performance across multiple state-of-the-art image understanding models
- Examines disparities in how different ethnic groups are represented and recognized
- Provides insights into fairness and representational bias in multimodal AI systems
