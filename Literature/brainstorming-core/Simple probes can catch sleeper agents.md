Anthropic is an AI safety and research company that's working to build reliable, interpretable, and steerable AI systems.

## Key points
- Linear probes of LLM internal activations can effectively detect sleeper agent behavior and misalignment
- Draws parallels between lie detection in neuroscience and deception detection in AI systems
- Suggests the geometric structure of dishonesty may be similar across brains and language models
- Proposes potential integration with neuroscience-based lie detection methods for improved alignment monitoring
- Raises the possibility of using brain-data derived deception vectors to detect LLM dishonesty
