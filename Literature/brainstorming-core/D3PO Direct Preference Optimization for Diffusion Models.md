## Summary
D3PO extends Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) to diffusion models, enabling preference-based learning without reinforcement learning. The method directly optimizes diffusion model objectives using paired preference data while maintaining computational efficiency.

## Key points
- First practical application of DPO-style preference optimization to diffusion models
- Avoids the computational overhead of reinforcement learning while learning from human preferences
- Demonstrates improvements in both image quality and alignment with user preferences
- Enables efficient fine-tuning of diffusion models with preference data
