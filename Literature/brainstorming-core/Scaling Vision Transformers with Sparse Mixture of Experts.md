## Summary
This paper explores the application of sparse Mixture of Experts (MoE) layers to Vision Transformers, proposing efficient scaling strategies that maintain model performance while reducing computational costs during inference.

## Key points
- Integrates sparse Mixture of Experts into Vision Transformer architectures for efficient scaling
- Demonstrates improved compute-to-performance trade-offs compared to dense scaling
- Addresses activation sparsity and routing efficiency in vision models
- Shows scalability benefits for large-scale vision tasks
