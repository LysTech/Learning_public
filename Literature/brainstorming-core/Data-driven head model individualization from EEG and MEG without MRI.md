## Summary
This methodology paper presents a data-driven approach for individualizing head models used in EEG and MEG analysis without requiring MRI scans. The work addresses optimal sensor placement and head model construction directly from electrophysiological data.

## Key points
- Data-driven head model individualization without MRI dependency
- Methodology for optimizing EEG/MEG sensor placement
- Potential applications for noise correction in electrophysiological recordings
- Enables head model construction from EEG and MEG data alone
