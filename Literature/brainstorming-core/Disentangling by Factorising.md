## Summary
This paper investigates disentanglement in neural representations by proposing a factorization-based approach to understanding how neural networks organize information. The work examines the relationship between disentanglement and interpretability in deep learning models.

## Key points
- Proposes factorization methods to disentangle neural representations
- Explores connections between disentanglement and model interpretability
- Evaluates approach on natural language processing tasks
- Demonstrates improved understanding of learned representations
