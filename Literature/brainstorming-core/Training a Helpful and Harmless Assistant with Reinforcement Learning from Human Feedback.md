## Summary
This paper describes Anthropic's approach to training helpful and harmless AI assistants using reinforcement learning from human feedback (RLHF). The work explores scaling laws for RLHF, constitutional AI methods, and empirical findings about alignment challenges when scaling to larger models.

## Key points
- Demonstrates scaling laws for RLHF training and identifies when larger models become harder to align
- Introduces Constitutional AI (CAI) as a method to reduce reliance on human feedback for training helpful and harmless assistants
- Documents the 'alignment tax' problem where helpfulness and harmlessness objectives can conflict at scale
- Provides empirical analysis of training dynamics and failure modes in RLHF for large language models
- Establishes benchmarks and evaluation methods for measuring progress on AI safety alignment
