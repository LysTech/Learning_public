## Summary
At Microsoft, we’re expanding AI capabilities by training small language models to achieve the kind of enhanced reasoning and comprehension typically found only in much larger models.

## Key points
- Small models trained on GPT-4 reasoning traces outperform larger models without such training
- Demonstrates effective knowledge distillation through alignment with stronger model reasoning patterns
- Shows that weaker systems benefit from learning how stronger systems think and approach problems
- Provides evidence for the efficiency of fine-tuning on reasoning tasks versus raw model scale
- Supports the case for open-source model development as fine-tuning provides competitive advantages
