## Summary
MindEye2: Shared-Subject Models Enable fMRI-To-Image With 1 Hour of Data

## Key points
- Reconstructs images from fMRI using shared-subject transfer learning models
- Achieves high-quality image generation with only ~1 hour of fMRI data per subject
- Uses diffusion models and CLIP embeddings for image reconstruction
- Demonstrates significant reduction in data requirements compared to prior fMRI decoding methods
